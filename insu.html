Health Insurance Plans

Ayushman Bharat

Ayushman Bharat is a healthcare initiative providing affordable services to economically vulnerable families, offering coverage of up to ₹5 lakhs per family for hospitalization.

  • Covers 1,500+ medical packages, including surgeries.
  • Cashless treatment at empaneled hospitals.
  • Reduces financial burden for low-income families.

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) is a government-backed insurance scheme designed to provide affordable coverage of a minimal premium of ₹12 per year

  • Coverage up to ₹2 lakhs for serious conditions.
  • Support for disability-related health issues
  • Annual premium of just ₹12 for easy accessibility.

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY) is a health insurance scheme aimed at providing affordable medical care to low-income families. It offers cashless treatment for medical services.

  • Health coverage up to ₹30,000 per family per year
  • Cashless hospitalization at public and private hospitals.
  • Low-cost premium with government assistance for BPL families
